Filmmaking

Filmmaking is a way of telling a story. It can have different purposes, such as entertaining, inspiring or educating.
The cast, the plot, the lightning and the audio editing are all aspects of filmmaking. These aspects, along with many
more, are used to create a compelling story that impacts the audience.

You can read the script of the film or someone can explain the film to you. However, no other method of storytelling
will impact you as much as a film can.
